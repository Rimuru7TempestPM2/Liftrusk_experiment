Operational Workflow Disclosures

Published to ensure compliance with the Consumer Protection Directives of Kenya.

1. Geographic Pathing Estimation: System mapping coordinates are isolated when pickup and terminal targets are submitted. The structural algorithmic node interfaces via geocoded distance APIs to convert geographic paths into a standard distance framework.

2. Curvature Multiplier Optimization: To accurately calculate local road geography, the linear vector model applies an exponential safety curvature correction buffer factor. This step generates a finalized, unalterable cost schedule prior to fleet notification.

3. Dynamic Energy Surcharge Indexing: The platform reads a baseline cost framework that tracks energy price indicators (as updated periodically by localized regulators like EPRA). This allows it to automatically scale delivery cost components up or down to ensure driver profit margins without changing structural base rate matrices.
